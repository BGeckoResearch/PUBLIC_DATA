# Weekly Execution Cadence (v0)

## Monday - Plan (60 min)
- Review last week's shipped artifacts.
- Pick **top 3 outcomes** for this week.
- Confirm owners + definition of done + blockers.
- Update Roadmap sheet.

## Daily - Standup (15 min)
1) Shipped yesterday (link)
2) Shipping today (link)
3) Blockers / asks

## Friday - Review (45 min)
- Demo what shipped (artifacts only).
- What surprised us?
- What to cut / double down on next week?
- Log key decisions in Decision_Log.xlsx.
